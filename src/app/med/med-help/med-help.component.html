How to use magnets?
⬆️ Tonification
Byol Magnet - Yellow side of the magnet will touch the skin
Chakra Magnet - Yellow inner side of the magnet will touch the skin
⬇️ Sedation
Byol Magnet - White side of the magnet will touch the skin
Chakra Magnet - White inner side of the magnet will touch the skin
